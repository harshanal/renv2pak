renv2pak

About: This tool is a workaround for scenarios where renv::restore() encounters issues. By using this application, you can convert a renv.lock file into a pak-based installation command. After installing your packages via pak, you may choose to take a new renv::snapshot() if needed to solidify your environment state.

Disclaimer: This might not be the best practice for strict reproducibility, but can help in tricky situations.
